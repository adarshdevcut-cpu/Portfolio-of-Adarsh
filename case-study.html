Books Vidya: E-Commerce Architecture

The Problem

Handling thousands of academic listings led to extreme DOM lag. The user experience was sluggish, causing high drop-off rates during search and checkout phases.

The Solution

I rebuilt the frontend logic to use asynchronous data fetching and lazy-loading for heavy assets. This achieved a "No-Lag" environment for students on both desktop and mobile devices.

Director BooksVidya.com
